Overall Aim of the Project

To analyze car sales, revenue, and vehicle performance metrics across different brands in order to identify top-performing manufacturers, understand demand patterns, and support data-driven business decisions in the automotive industry.

Dashboard 1 – Sales & Revenue Performance Analysis
Aim

The aim of Dashboard 1 is to evaluate overall sales and revenue performance by car brand. It helps identify top-selling brands, compare total sales and revenue contribution, and analyze pricing trends to understand which brands drive the highest business value.

Dashboard 2 – Vehicle Performance & Efficiency Analysis
Aim

The aim of Dashboard 2 is to analyze vehicle performance and efficiency metrics such as horsepower and fuel efficiency across brands. This dashboard helps understand the relationship between performance, efficiency, and market positioning of different car manufacturers.

Summary

This project uses interactive dashboards to analyze car sales, revenue, and vehicle performance, enabling better insights into brand performance, customer preferences, and market trends.
